Game of War

Press on your decks to play!





How to play War

1. Click on your respective decks to begin the game
2. Each player will send one card into the middle pile.
3. Whoever has the higher card collects both cards in the middle.
4. If both players send a card into the middle that are of the
same value, then each player must four cards and whoever has
the highest fourth card wins the cards played in the middle.
5. The game continues until one player has ran out of cards to play,
which will result in that player losing.
Have Fun!
